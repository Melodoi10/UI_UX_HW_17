Fish and Wildlife Service Website Redesign

Revitalizing conservation with a user-friendly design. As part of a team project, we redesigned the Fish and Wildlife Service website to create a more engaging, intuitive experience for users. Our goal was to make important conservation information and resources easier to access, with a clean and structured layout. By improving navigation, adding eye-catching visuals, and focusing on usability, our redesign helps users explore wildlife initiatives, learn about protected species, and get involved in conservation efforts more easily.
